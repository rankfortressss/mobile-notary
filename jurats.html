Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Jurat?
A Jurat is a sworn statement that certifies the identity of the signer and that the document was signed in the presence of a Notary Public.
How can I get a Jurat signed using Mobile Notary services?
Mobile Notary services provide an efficient, secure, and convenient way to have documents notarized remotely by connecting you with an experienced mobile notary who can come to your location or meet virtually online.
Is a Jurat valid if it's signed by a Mobile Notary?
Yes, Jurats are legally binding when they are signed by a qualified Mobile Notary in accordance with state laws and regulations.
