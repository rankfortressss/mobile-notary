Frequently Asked Questions

What type of background check do you provide?
We provide a comprehensive background check that includes a criminal record search, driving history and consumer credit report.
How long does the background check process take?
The entire process takes approximately two business days to complete.
Is the background check secure and confidential?
Yes, all the information collected during the background check is securely stored and all personal information is kept strictly confidential.
Are there any additional fees associated with the background check?
No, there are no additional fees associated with obtaining a mobile notary's background check.
Are there any specific requirements for becoming a mobile notary?
Yes, most states require applicants to pass a written exam in order to become licensed as a mobile notary public.
